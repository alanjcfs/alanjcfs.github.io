I watched the last twenty minutes of the debate after I came home.
This came after a day at work where I felt lost and unproductive.

"Welcome to the developer's life," said my boss, when I responded haltingly
of how finished the models were. I said that I was finished and not finished.

I don't know how it is that I know and do not know. I feel as if my brain
is trying to slither—without a muscle—around some sharp right-angle
corner, a line drawing on a graph paper.
