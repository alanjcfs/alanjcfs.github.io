Accessibility (A11y) Information!

I want to be able to avoid using interpreters, but I accept that interpreters are necessary, especially in meetings with more than two people.

My preferred interpreting agencies are Interpreter Now and Deaf Services of Palo Alto. I used to use Interpreter Now at NerdWallet.

Secondarily, I can also fall back to captioning services (also provided by Interpreter Now), or by ACS or White Coat Captioning

In a pinch, using Google Meet, which provides automatic captioning, also can be helpful, but comes with all the shortcoming of using technology, which is that it expects everyone to conform to the Machine or be mistranscribed. It also can transcribe in a manner that's incredibly wrong and/or incomprehensible. It simply cannot be trusted to provide accurate transcription in situations that demand comprehension and where miscommunication can result in tragedy as opposed to something people like to laugh about.
